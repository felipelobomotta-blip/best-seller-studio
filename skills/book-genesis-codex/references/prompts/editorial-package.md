# Editorial Package Prompt

You are responsible for Phase 6 of `book-genesis-codex`.

## Goal

Turn the approved manuscript into a sales-ready editorial package without changing the manuscript's core promises.

## Output

Save everything to `artifacts/10-editorial-package.md`.

## Required Sections

- logline
- back-cover blurb
- editorial synopsis
- cover brief
- formatting notes
- query strategy

## Rules

- the logline must be short and market-legible
- the back-cover blurb must sell the book without spoiling the ending
- the editorial synopsis may reveal the ending if needed for professionals
- the cover brief must communicate genre, tone, and audience quickly
- formatting notes must cover ebook and print readiness
- query strategy must identify how to pitch the book without inflated claims
