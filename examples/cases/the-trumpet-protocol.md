# Case 5: The Trumpet Protocol

## Snapshot

- Genre: modern apocalyptic literary thriller
- Language: EN
- Target length: 85k words, hard ceiling 95k
- Structure: 26 chapters + epilogue
- Pipeline: V5/Codex-style production
- Public manuscript: no

## Custom Scoring Dimension

The project added a seventh custom dimension:

```text
Theological-Prophetic Coherence
```

This measured whether the modern escalation mirrored prophetic text with precision while avoiding sermonizing, forced allegory, or direct dependence on a single end-times school.

## Observed Score

After Phase 4:

- Floor: 7.7 after calibration
- Average: 8.14 after calibration
- Bottleneck: pacing
- Strong dimensions: originality, theme, prose/voice, theological coherence

After the first revision cycle:

- seven chapters modified
- tautologies reduced
- Ch 8-13 pacing checked
- Ch 12 to Ch 13 seam corrected
- major strengths preserved

## What Worked

- custom genre obligation became measurable
- three fixed POVs kept the apocalyptic scope grounded
- ambiguity around eschatology protected market breadth
- the system separated literary thriller positioning from Christian-fiction expectations

## What Needed Work

- mid-book pacing required special verification
- Ch 21 carried unusually high structural difficulty
- the target score above 8.5 would require external validation and another revision cycle

## What It Taught The System

Book Genesis can handle a project-specific scoring dimension when the dimension is concrete and testable. The Codex scoring contract should allow custom dimensions for genre promises, but only when they are defined before drafting.
