# Case 9: Agenda 2030

## Snapshot

- Genre: epic fantasy / sci-fi hybrid / apocalyptic thriller
- Language: EN
- Target length: 135k-150k words
- Pipeline: portable-agent foundation and RAG calibration
- Public manuscript: no

## Custom Scoring Dimension

```text
Entity Coherence
```

This measures whether emerging entities follow internal logic without turning the book into exposition.

## Why It Matters

Agenda 2030 is a scale test. It asks the system to coordinate collapse fiction, entity emergence, resistance-cell plotting, philosophical stakes, and multi-POV structure without losing causal clarity.

## What The Pipeline Had To Control

- foundation before drafting
- consistent entity logic
- dark literary tone plus action readability
- three fixed POVs
- large-scale world change without pure explanation

## What It Taught The System

For large speculative concepts, the pipeline needs calibration before prose. The book's risk is not a weak scene; it is incoherent ontology. That makes early assumptions, architecture, and custom scoring dimensions load-bearing.
