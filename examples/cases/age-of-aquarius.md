# Case 2: Age of Aquarius

## Snapshot

- Genre: hermetic fantasy
- Language: EN
- Approximate length: 97k words
- Pipeline: V3/V4-style iterative evaluation
- Public artifacts: yes, under `examples/age-of-aquarius/`

## Why It Matters

Age of Aquarius showed the upside and the danger of internal scoring. The system could drive the manuscript toward a high Genesis Score by iterating on structure, theme, and emotional architecture. That was useful, but it also made the team more aware that internal scores need external calibration.

## What Worked

- strong macro-structure
- alchemical arc mapping
- coherent outline across 28 chapters
- complete synopsis package
- strong internal Genesis Score report

## What It Taught The System

This case helped separate "passes the rubric" from "lands with readers." It became part of the evidence behind the later universal-core rule: audit first, score second, and do not let score-chasing dominate drafting.
