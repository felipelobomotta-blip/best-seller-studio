# Case 1: Protocolo Nao Encontrado

## Snapshot

- Genre: memoir / generational essay / acid self-help
- Language: PT-BR
- Approximate length: 68k-71k words
- Pipeline: early simpler Book Genesis flow
- Public artifacts: yes, under `examples/protocolo-nao-encontrado/`

## Why It Matters

This case is the project that exposed the most important Book Genesis lesson: fewer constraints can produce more alive prose. The system had not yet grown into the full V4/V5 network, so the writer carried fewer simultaneous obligations.

The result scored strongly with external readers compared with later, heavier pipeline runs.

## What Worked

- strong authorial voice
- clear emotional through-line
- marketable memoir/essay positioning
- durable editorial package artifacts
- multilingual synopsis handoff

## What It Taught The System

The case became evidence against the assumption that more agents always improve a book. It directly influenced the later V5 Craft Mode and the portable core's move toward drafting first, judging later.
